About Me

Hello! I am Soumay Soni, a passionate and dedicated front-end developer with a strong foundation in React.js and modern web development technologies. As a recent graduate with a Bachelor of Technology in Electrical Engineering from Rajkiye Engineering College Kannuaj, I have honed my skills through self made projects and continuous self-learning. My journey in web development began with a curiosity about how websites are built and a desire to create seamless user experiences. I quickly found my niche in front-end development, where I can combine my creativity with technical skills to build interactive and responsive web applications.

I have gained hands-on experience with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React.js, along with other tools and frameworks like Bootstrap and Git. I have developed several projects that showcase my ability to design and implement user-friendly interfaces, solve complex problems, and collaborate effectively in team environments. I am always eager to learn and adapt to new challenges, and I am committed to staying updated with the latest industry trends and best practices. My goal is to contribute to innovative projects that make a meaningful impact, and I am excited about the opportunity to bring my skills and enthusiasm to your team.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I look forward to connecting with you and exploring how we can work together to create exceptional web experiences.
